Fyst

A fitness app for wearables that tracks your daily routine and provides a personalised exercise experience to keep you motivated and healthy.







Overview

The current lifestyle patterns among young working professionals living in Indian cities is leading to various health problems such as high-BP, obesity, diabetes, etc. However, every problem is tied to a lot of variables in the system such as individuals, organisations, governments, etc. which affect their health directly or indirectly. The project aims to study the ecosystem around the user, his behaviours and his needs, to provide necessary design intervention for a healthy life.

Research
REPORTS

In order to verify whether the problem is serious, we looked at news reports, articles and research papers to find out the stats and to understand the gravity of this problem. The findings from the study suggested that lifestyle diseases are emerging as one of the serious health problem around the world, especially among young professionals in India.

 

 
UN Sustainable Development Goal

The United Nations - Sustainable Development Goal 3 targets to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages.
The target in context, by 2030, reduce by one third premature mortality from noncommunicable diseases through prevention and treatment and promote mental health and well-being

causes

The primary causes of lifestyle diseases among young adults, found by various reports, have been cited as stress, lack of exercise, unhealthy diet and smoking.

Persona

For the qualitative research, we prepared a questionnaire and interviewed 7 participants to get a first-hand perspective of the activities, causes, effects and the stakeholders around the user. It lead me to narrow down to one major persona of a young working professional to go ahead and design for

AKHIL KUMAR
28 Years, Single, IT Professional

PSYCHOGRAPHICS
  • Earns good salary. Spendthrift.
  • Loves to eat. Parties hard.
  • Ambitious. Impatient.
  • Cares about his family.
TECHNOGRAPHICS
  • Uses Macbook and iPad.
  • Also has an expensive smartphone.
PAIN POINTS
  • Does not feel motivated.
  • Time constraints.
  • Thinks exercise is boring.
  • Prefers work over health.
NEEDS
  • Wants to get fit
  • Wants to look good

ECOSYSTEM

The ecosystem around a young working professional talks about his interactions and behaviours with things, individuals and organisations in the system. It connects the user with the other stakeholders in the system and their role.

Design

This project had its genesis in an older product developed by IBM Cloud Mobile which was a web-based Rapid Mobile App Developer(RMAD) tool. It assisted the enterprise mobile app developers to consume the services offered, by letting it integrate pre-built components such as using datasets, adding security into the mobile app being built. The intention for the new project was to let enterprise citizen developers consume Mobile Foundation service through a desktop-tool and create a digital app.

The tool should also be able to integrate AI capabilities into the app. Multi-channel app development meant 'Design once, develop many' where the user starts creating the app for a single channel(ex. iOS) and can develop it for many(ex. Android, Web, PWA, etc)
